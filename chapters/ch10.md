# Vaccine Damage Payment Scheme

## Introduction

The Vaccine Damage Payment Scheme (VDPS) provides a single, tax-free payment to people (or their families) who have suffered severe mental and/or physical disablement as a result of immunisation against certain diseases.

## Further information and how to claim

You can obtain more information about eligibility for the VDPS and how to claim at: https://www.gov.uk/vaccine-damage-payment
